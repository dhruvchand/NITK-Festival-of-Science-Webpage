Welcome to NITK Festival of Science

NFS or the NITK Festival of Science is an initiative by NITK, through which the institute wishes to excite curiosity and a passion for science among urban & rural school students with fun, hands-on, minds-on learning. Through this venture, NITK aims to encourage budding young scientists to use their senses and critical thinking skills to explore science, math, and engineering concepts in an exciting hands-on environment.
